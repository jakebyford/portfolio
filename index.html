About Me

“Sometimes it is the very people who no one imagines anything of who do the things no one can imagine.”

- Alan Turing

The vision for my journey was inspired by my mother, a healthcare employee who worked as a mammography technician in New Jersey. Raised on the law of attraction, my mom taught me to have the conscious ability to build beautiful ideas, live in harmony through positive energy, and attract the good that I desire.

When I was 13 years old, the struggles from the 2009 United States financial crisis ultimately aligned my need for financial knowledge. During my time as a student, I witnessed firsthand the struggle my family had in choosing between saving and spending money. The desire to provide for my family ignited a passion within me, and with some guidance, I decided to study Finance. Years later, working two jobs, I virtually graduated Magna Cum Laude from Kean University in Union, New Jersey, obtaining my Bachelor’s Degree in Finance May 29th, 2020. Although graduating in the midst of a global pandemic, the dismal job market triggered an idea I once had, stemming from advice given to me a year prior from a head executive. I quickly decided to take advantage of this stay-at-home order and enroll in a Data Science Bootcamp through Rutgers University to further invest in my knowledge.

Since 2018, I have grown organically and exponentially becoming one of the best FinTech candidates to enter the current job market. My entrepreneurial achievements are being recognized and my success stands as inspiration for others to take risks in pursuit of their passion. Focusing on operating with quality and integrity, one of my near future ambitions is to develop a “virtual financial analyst” interface capable of autonomously scraping company financial data and updating ratios to predict better investment decisions.
